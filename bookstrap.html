Football is a very popular sport that is played at high schools, colleges, and professional stadiums. It involves running, passing, kicking, and body contact. A football game is played between two teams of 11 players each. A team scores points by moving a ball across the other team's goal line. It is played at international level by teams belonging to various countries. Unlike other sports, the football teams of various counties have been given unique names by which they are popular among the football fans. Football is one of the most loved games worldwide. To play football, you need two teams, each with 11 players. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net. Players use their feet to kick the ball and pass it to teammates. The game consists of two halves, each lasting 45 minutes. Football is a game that is heartily played in any condition. It can be played in rain, snowfalls, open spring fields, etc. This versatility of the game allows you not to miss any match under any kind of weather conditions.
